from Terraform vs. CloudFormation, Heat, etc.

與 CloudFormation, Heat類似，但是有以下兩點優點：

Enabling multiple providers and services to be combined and composed.

允許同時架構混合系統，如 AWS + OpenStack。

Terraform also separates the planning phase from the execution phase.

可以透過 plan 階段視覺化執行項目，了解規劃是否正確。

Installation

1. 下載zip檔案

wget https://releases.hashicorp.com/terraform/0.12.2/terraform_0.12.2_linux_amd64.zip

2. 解壓縮

unzip terraform_0.12.2_linux_amd64.zip

3. 搬移到某個地方

move terraform xxxxx

4. 在 .profile設定PATH參數

export PATH="$PATH:xxxxx"
